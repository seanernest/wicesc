About Daily HUMSS.

Daily HUMSS is a class project designed to partially meet the requirements of Work Immersion and Community Engagement, Solidarity, and Citizenship subjects.

Our Purpose

The purpose of this website is to be able to gather knowledge in to one, with the help of this website. Daily HUMSS purpose is to educate the brilliant minds of the readers and to show and express what the country Philippines is currently facing, and using this website showcase the students their best works, and their advocacies to further more the scope and share the knowledge of the Grade 12 HUMSS students of STEFTI

The Contributors

This entire website is built on top of a template that has been extensively modified and recoded by Sean Ernest Siega who also serves as this website's editor and programmer.

The various articles featured on this website are courtesy of the students of 12-Goodness Batch 2022. The rights of the works that are featured on this website belong to their authors.

The editor would like to thank everyone who participated and submitted on time.
